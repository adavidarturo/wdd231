About Me

My name is David Anampa, and I’m passionate about understanding how technology, data, and economics connect to shape the modern world. I started my academic path through BYU Pathway, studying web and computer programming with a focus on software and data development. Over time, I discovered my strongest interests lie in business, finance, and global economics, where I can combine analytical thinking with creativity to solve real-world problems. I enjoy learning how systems work — from financial markets to political and technological trends — and how data can drive smarter decisions. I’m currently expanding my skills in JavaScript, databases, and web design, while preparing for future studies in business management and finance. Outside of academics, I’m dedicated to fitness, discipline, and self-improvement. I believe success comes from consistency, balance, and resilience. My goal is to grow into a professional who not only understands business and technology but also the human and cultural side behind every innovation.
