MMA Academy – "The Warrior Path"

Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

MMA Academy – The Warrior Path

This name reflects the journey of young people who want to train in multiple martial arts disciplines with the best instructors and equipment.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an attractive and functional hub for a fictional MMA gym. It will present the philosophy of training, list the disciplines offered, and provide subscription levels with a contact form for new members.

Scenarios

  • What martial arts disciplines can I train at MMA Academy?
  • How can I subscribe and what levels of training are available?
  • Who are the instructors and what experience do they have?

Color Scheme

Chosen colors:

  • Dark Red (#8B0000) – headings and accents
  • Bright Red (#E63946) – highlights and buttons
  • Light Background (#F8F9FA) – page background

Typography

Fonts selected:

  • Roboto – for body text (clean and modern)
  • Oswald – for headings (bold and impactful)

Wireframe

Home Page Layout:

  • Mobile: Hero banner on top, followed by sections stacked vertically.
  • Desktop: Hero banner across the top, disciplines in a grid, subscription/contact side by side.
